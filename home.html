The Akilah institute was founded in 2008 by
Elizabeth Dearborn Hughes and Dave hughes.
Elizabeth Hughes moved to Rwanda
after graduating from venderbilt university in 2006.
After volunteering with several grassroots initiatives,
she co-founded Amani Africa to provide scholarships to street
children

The akilah is a non profit college for women in
Kigali Rwanda.
It is the first college for women in the country.
The institute offers three years diplomas
in enterpreneurship, hospitality management, and information
systems.
Akilah is accredited through Rwanda's Ministry of Education
